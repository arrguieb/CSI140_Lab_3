What I listen to when studying

Different types of noises

There are 3 main types
Brown Description Benefits
  • deep sound with bass-like tone
  • can help lower symptoms of ringing in ears
  • similar to steady heavy rainfall
  • relaxation and mindfulness
  • stress relief
  • improved sleep quality
White Description Benefits
  • intense and high-pitched like a fan, air conditioner, or vacuum
  • often called "broadband noise"
  • the most popular one
  • can keep the brain focused
  • improved sleep quality
  • reduced stress
Pink Description Benefits
  • may be heard called ambient noise
  • lower pitch than white noise, but higher pitch than brown noise
  • sounds like steady rain, wind rustling through trees, or waves on a beach
  • enhances sleep and memory
  • improved concentration
  • used to treat hearing disorder and tinnitus
